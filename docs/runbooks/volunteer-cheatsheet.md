## How every station works (the same 4 steps)

1. **Scan** the kid's wristband QR with the camera (or paste the code into the box if the camera struggles).
2. Tap **"Look up"** — the kid's card appears with their name, perk balance, and a colored consent banner (green = photos OK, red = no photos).
3. **Do your station's action** (tap the right button, see your section below).
4. Wait for the green **"✨ ..."** success toast. Tap **"Scan next wristband"** to move on.

If the card border is **red**, do not photograph this kid. Period.
If the card has a **yellow allergy banner**, read it before handing them food or letting them visit the kitchen.

## 👋 Check-in

**Your job:** Greet the family at the jail set, take the mugshot, send them in.

**In the app:**
1. Scan kid's QR. (Have siblings? Scan each one.)
2. Type your name in "Your name (staff)".
3. Camera shows the jail viewfinder. Frame the family at the jail set. Tap **"📸 Take mugshot"**. Wait for "Mugshot saved".
4. Pick the dropoff: **Both parents / One parent / Grandparent / Other approved adult**.
5. Tap the big magenta **"Check In"** button.
6. Green toast = done. Hand them a wristband if not already on. Send them off.

**If the card is red (no photos):** A red box says "🚫 No photo consent — mugshot is skipped." That's fine. Just pick the dropoff and tap Check In. Skip the photo entirely.

**If the kid is already checked in:** Yellow box says when. Don't double check-in. Send them on.

## 🚪 Check-out

**Your job:** Make sure the kid leaves with someone on their approved list.

**In the app:**
1. Scan kid's QR.
2. The app shows a list of pre-approved pickup people: primary parent, secondary parent, plus anyone the family added (Grandma, Aunt, etc.). **Tap the person who's actually here.**
3. Type your name in "Your name (staff)".
4. Tap mint-green **"Release to selected"**.
5. Green toast = the kid is officially released and the keepsake-email story for them starts generating in the background.

**If the pickup person is NOT on the list:** STOP. Do not release the child. Walk to the head check-out volunteer or call the admin. The parent needs to verbally authorize over the phone, and an admin must override.

**If the kid isn't checked in yet:** Yellow box. Send them to the check-in table first.

## 🥤 Drinks (metered — 2 per kid)

**Your job:** Pour the drink, mark it on their wristband.

**In the app:**
1. Scan kid's QR.
2. The card shows their **drinks remaining** (cyan chip). Should be 2, 1, or 0.
3. Tap cyan **"Redeem drink ticket"**.
4. Green toast: "Drink redeemed — N left." Pour the drink. Done.

**If they have 0 left:** Red banner says "No drink tickets left on this wristband." The button is disabled. Send the parent to the admin (Brian) — admin can comp extra tickets from the child detail page.

## 🚨 Jail (metered — 3 sends OR passes, total)

**Your job:** Lock 'em up, or use a pass to spring a kid out.

**In the app:**
1. Scan kid's QR.
2. The card shows **jail / pass remaining** (magenta chip). Same bucket — sends and passes both pull from the same 3.
3. Pick **"🚨 Send to jail"** or **"🗝️ Use pass"**.
4. Type your name (optional).
5. Tap **"Send to jail"** or **"Use get-out pass"**.
6. Green toast confirms it.

**If they have 0 left:** Red banner. Button disabled. They're out for the rest of the night — no reload for jail tickets. Politely tell them.

**Note:** Check-in already takes a jail-style mugshot photo separately. The "Send to jail" action here just logs a visit — no second photo is needed.

## ✨ Photo (scan-then-shoot)

**Your job:** Capture posed photos at any of the booth-style stations.

**In the app:**
1. Scan **every kid in the frame** — each one becomes a chip at the top.
2. Type your name (optional).
3. Allow the camera. Frame the shot. Tap big magenta **"📸 Shutter (N)"** where N is how many kids are in frame.
4. Green toast: "✨ Uploaded · tagged N kids." Photo is saved against every kid you scanned.
5. Want to keep shooting the same group? Just tap shutter again. Want a different group? Tap the ✕ on each chip to remove them, then scan new kids.

**If a kid in the batch has NO photo consent:** A full-screen red modal blocks the shutter. Tap "Remove no-consent kids" — they vanish from the chips and you can shoot the rest. Or ask the kid to step out of frame.

## 📸 Roaming photographer (no scanning needed)

**Your job:** Shoot candids around the room. The app figures out who's in each photo.

**In the app:**
1. Type your name (optional).
2. Allow camera. Aim. Tap purple **"📸 Shutter"**.
3. The shot appears in "Recent shots" below: uploading → analyzing → "✅ tagged [name]" / "🔍 pending review" / "❓ unmatched".
4. Just keep shooting. Don't wait between shots.

**Important:** Only kids whose parents opted in to face matching get auto-tagged. If a photo says "unmatched", the admin will review later and tag manually. That's normal — don't worry about it.

## If something goes wrong

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| App is slow | Wait 5 seconds and try again. Most actions are quick — slowness usually means the network is choking, not the app. |
| QR won't scan (camera autofocus struggling, blacklight is rough on cameras) | Type the code manually into the input box below the camera. Every station has one. |
| Kid forgot or lost their wristband | Call the admin (Brian). Admin can look the kid up by name in `/admin/children` and reissue or use a temporary code. |
| Card says "0 left" but the kid swears they have more | Call the admin. Admin can comp tickets via the child detail page. |
| Camera doesn't open | Refresh the page (you may need to log back in). On iPhone Safari: Settings → Safari → Camera → set to Allow. |
| You lost the station picker (it dumped you to "no station selected") | Tap **"Pick a station"** and pick yours again. The app remembers, you don't have to keep doing this all night. |
| Station shows yellow "Not checked in yet" warning when you scan | Send the kid to the **Check-in** table first. Nothing else works until they're checked in. |
| You accidentally tapped the wrong action and it went through | Call the admin. They can reverse most things from the child detail page. |

---

## Who to find for what

- **App not working / scanner broken / weird error:** Brian (cell number on a sticker on your tablet).
- **Allergy emergency or any medical issue:** Call 911 first. Then alert the head of school.
- **Lost child:** Head check-out volunteer + admin together. Sweep stations.
- **Pickup person not on the approved list:** Head check-out runs the override. Do **not** release the child without it. No exceptions even if the kid begs.
- **Parent disputes a ticket count or wants a comp:** Send them to the admin / cash box. Don't argue with parents.
- **A kid wandered into the wrong age group / area:** Walk them back. No app action needed.
