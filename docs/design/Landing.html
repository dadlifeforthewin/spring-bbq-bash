05Parent questions, answered

Do I have to stay?

Nope. As long as your child is 2 or older and has a ticket, drop them off and go have fun with the other adults.

Can I buy extra drinks?

Each child already gets 2 premium drinks + their own water bottle — more than enough. Basic lemonade + water are free all night, too.

What if my kid has allergies?

Tell us on the form. Every wristband scan shows allergies to the volunteer before they hand over food.

What about photos of my kid?

You control it. Three toggles on the form: the PTO album, the day-after surprise, or neither.
